Ingredient:
Egg 1
Big Onion 1
Garlic 4
Small red onion 2
Chili 3
Salt (1/4 tea spoon)
soya sauce (5 tea spoon)
Oil

Direction:
1- Cut all onion and chili into small pieces
2- Break the egg in bowl, put in salt and hit the egg for a minute.
3- Heat the oil in frying pan.
4- When the oil is hot enough, fry all onion and chili until its smell good.
5- Add soya sauce then stir slowly.
6- When its smell really good, add the egg and stir slowly.
7- When done you can eat with hot rice.
